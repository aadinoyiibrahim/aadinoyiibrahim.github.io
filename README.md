I am currently a Ph.D. student at International Max Planck Research School for Intelligent systems (MPI-IS and Uni-Tuebingen). 
The main focus of my research is routing problems with machine learning, combining approaches originating from optimal transport and biologically inspired networks. In particular, I am interested in understanding how different parameters help to understand 
									the traffic dynamics on multilayer networks. The applications of my research include extracting the user's optimal trajectory 
									from transportation networks and analysing how optimal topological properties vary in different optimization settings.
